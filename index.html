About Us

We are a company that provides prepaid medicine services focused on pets through insurance policies that cover the following areas: medical illnesses, accidents and damages to third parties, all with the purpose to protect pets and their owners' pockets.

Our Mission

We care about protecting home pets against any eventuality in order to their owners feel supported by us when it comes to unbudgeted expenses. We will also pay the vet bills, and you only have to worry about your pet's recovery.

Our Vision

We want to be an internationally recognized company and serve as a benchmark in the provision of prepaid medicine services for pets, demonstrating commitment, efficiency, solidarity with animals and honesty in all our processes.
